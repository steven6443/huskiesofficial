Is the Cheat Sheet really free?
Completely. No cost, no trial, no card. Enter your name and email and the whole 10-page field guide is yours — our way of welcoming you to the pack.
What's actually inside it?
A 10-page guide covering blowing-coat survival, escape-proofing, the vocalization decoder, the exercise formula, heat & climate safety, and daily care with a fill-in vet quick-reference — all drawn from our community and reputable veterinary sources.
How do I get it after signing up?
Check your inbox for a confirmation email and click to confirm (this keeps bots out). The moment you do, your Cheat Sheet PDF lands in your inbox to download and keep.
Will you spam me?
Never. You'll get the guide plus the occasional genuinely useful email from the pack — tips, new resources, community highlights. Unsubscribe in one click anytime, and we never sell or share your info.
Do I have to be on Facebook to join?
Not at all. The Cheat Sheet comes by email. Our Facebook group, page, and Instagram are there if you want the day-to-day community — but they're entirely optional.
Is this only for new husky owners?
Both. The guide reframes the basics for first-timers and still surprises seasoned owners — especially the escape-proofing checklist and the vocalization decoder.
